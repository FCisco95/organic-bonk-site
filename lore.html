The Organic Lore

The story of $ORG told in chapters.

From the chaos of launch to the strength of community — read how a joke became a movement. Scroll or use the right-side nav to jump between chapters.

CHAPTER 1 · LAUNCH SPARKS

The accidental birth and the first day that changed everything.

August 11th, 2025. What started as a joke on LetsBonk turned into the spark of something bigger. A simple launch, meant as a sarcastic test, was thrown into chaos when my wallet got flagged as if I was the developer of Graphite Protocol. Suddenly, bots and snipers flooded in. The chart went wild. People panicked, some reached out in anger, others in confusion.

That was the moment I realized: I wasn’t the one to blame, but I also couldn’t just walk away. Everyone was losing. So, I stepped up. I made it clear: I am not the dev of GP. I am here, with you. And from that very day, $ORG became more than just a meme—it became a movement.

CHAPTER 2 · TAKEOVER & THE SECOND PUMP

How the community took control and we rode another wave.

When the dust settled, the community asked me to lead. I even paid for the community takeover on DEXScreener to make it official. What followed was our second big pump—a moment of pure energy. But once again, the snipers struck, selling heavily and dragging us down.

Instead of quitting, we pushed on. We weren’t here for quick flips or artificial hype. We were here to grow organically.

CHAPTER 3 · BUILDING FROM THE LOWS

Persistence, presence, and the slow work of rebuilding.

For almost a month, the chart drifted into the bottom range. Many projects would have died there, but not us. I kept buying, kept engaging, kept searching for ways to bring real value. I even showed up at in-real-life crypto events, talking about $ORG, proving that the story wasn’t over.

Through persistence, memes, and constant transparency, the flame stayed alive. What started as sarcasm slowly transformed into resilience.

CHAPTER 4 · THE RETURN OF THE COMMUNITY

A comeback fueled by old friends and new energy.

Then came the comeback. Old members returned, new faces joined, and even the Chinese community rallied with us. Energy shifted. Hope resurfaced. We weren’t just surviving anymore—we were building again.

This new website you’re reading is proof of that. A clear structure, an evolving roadmap, and a vision that extends far beyond memes.

CHAPTER 5 · WRITING THE ENDING TOGETHER

How the community writes the final chapter.

$ORG is no longer my experiment. It’s no longer “Cisco’s coin.” It’s a community project where anyone can contribute. If you want to build, build. If you want to create memes, create. If you have ideas, the community decides together.

We’re not against KOLs or listings, but we won’t fake it. We don’t want bots or inorganic hype. The community must win first. Growth comes from within.

This lore doesn’t end with me—it ends with us. Together, we will write the final chapter of $ORG. From the chaos of day one to the heights we aim to reach in this next alt-season, the story remains the same: Organic from here.
